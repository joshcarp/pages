Registration

Can I register for individual days?

Yes. Email alumni@pearsoncollege.ca. Payment options: Interac e-transfer to accountsreceivable@pearsoncollege.ca or PayPal paypalcan@pearsoncollege.ca.

  • 20-Year Single day off-site: $151.20
  • 20-Year Full day onsite: $188.10
  • Child pricing off-site: $125.38
  • Child pricing onsite: $159.62
Can I bring my spouse/partner and children?

Yes. Please notify us of needs (car seats, life jackets, highchairs) in advance. Costs for rentals are borne by registrants.

Refunds and cancellations

Full refund minus $25 processing until July 15. No refunds after.

Travel & Transportation

Some countries require an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter Canada.
Ground transportation to campus

Complimentary shuttle with advance request (before June 28).

  • Pick-ups: 12:00 Swartz Bay, 13:00 YYJ, 14:00 Royal BC Museum
  • Departures: 08:00 Ferry/YYJ, 10:00 Royal BC Museum
What to bring

Layers for 15–25°C days and 10–15°C evenings; walking shoes; rain gear; sunscreen; insect repellent.

Accommodation

Rooms & check-in

Arrive after 12:00 on Day 1. Check-out by 10:00 on the last day. Check-in at the Dining Hall registration desk.

Accessibility

Accessible options include Stoki House Room 6, ramps, shower chairs, and accessible washrooms in key buildings.

Food & Facilities

Meals, bar, and campus shopping

See the schedule for meal times. Bar open each evening; cash (CAD) and cards accepted. College swag available during lunch and dinner.
